# ISOSTASY | Sovereign Spatial Indexing Framework

## Executive Summary
ISOSTASY is a high-performance, open-source spatial discretization engine designed to provide a resilient foundation for autonomous systems and critical infrastructure. Forked from MapYourGrid, ISOSTASY abstracts complex environmental data into deterministic grid structures, enabling seamless spatial reasoning for Social AI and inclusive robotics. Our mission is to ensure digital sovereignty by providing an auditable, transparent, and vendor-neutral layer for spatial intelligence.

## Core Architecture Principles
* **Interoperability by Design:** Adheres to open geospatial standards to ensure compatibility across diverse robotic platforms and AI ecosystems.
* **Security-First Engineering:** Implements strict data validation and modular isolation to mitigate injection risks and ensure system integrity in production environments.
* **Systemic Resilience:** Optimized for long-term stability and performance under high-concurrency workloads, ensuring deterministic outcomes in mission-critical scenarios.

## Compliance & Sovereignty
ISOSTASY is engineered with European digital sovereignty at its core. 
* **Regulatory Alignment:** Designed to meet the transparency requirements of the EU AI Act.
* **Data Governance:** Supports local-first processing to ensure compliance with GDPR and high-level institutional security frameworks.
* **Open Infrastructure:** Built exclusively on open-source dependencies to prevent vendor lock-in and ensure long-term public interest viability.
